Chicken Udon Noodle Soup

Information

A super simple one-pot chicken udon soup that guarantees maximum satisfaction with beautifully seared chicken, rich soup, thick noodles, and tender vegetables.

  • Cooking time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes
  • Preparation time: 20 minutes
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 (32 fluid ounce) containers chicken stock
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 large cooked skinless, boneless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 head bok boy, chopped
  • 1.4 cup dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 (7 ounce) packages dried udon noodles
  • 1/2 cup mung bean sprouts
  • 1 green onion, sliced diagonally
  • 2 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh coriander

Directions

  1. Bring chicken stock, garlic, ginger, and chili powder to a boil in a pot over medium-high heat. Add chicken, bok choy, and mushrooms; let simmer lightly for 3 minutes. Add noodles and cook soup for 4 minutes more.
  2. Pour soup mixture evenly into 2 soup bowls. Place mung beans on top in the center of each bowl, with green onion placed neatly on top. Sprinkle dried onions and coriander on top.

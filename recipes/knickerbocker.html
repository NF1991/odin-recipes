Strawberry Knickerbocker Glory

Information

Knickerbocker glory, the dessert of childhood dreams! Very simple to prepare but impressive-looking when assembled, these will be loved by children and adults alike.

  • Cooking time: None
  • Total time: Less than 30 minutes
  • Preparation time: Less than 30 minutes
  • Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1lb fresh raspberries
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled, stone removed, diced
  • 5 1/2oz strawberries
  • 12 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1oz pistachos, coarsley chopped
